Indian Curry

Curry with many spices

Among the spices used in Indian curries, chili pepper (cayenne pepper), black pepper, and green pepper are the ones that add spiciness. Chili pepper is made from dried red pepper and black pepper is spiced from dried peppercorns. Both of these spices are sometimes used in "garam masala," a mixed spice in Indian cuisine. Green peppers are also peppers before they are fully ripe and are often used as an ingredient in South Indian curries. Indian curries that use hot spices can fully enjoy the stimulating spiciness.
